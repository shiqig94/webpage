VENDOR INFORMATION

Repair and Preservation Network's success in providing a full suite of effective and efficient services to industry’s top servicers and lenders has largely resulted from our excellent vendor network. We embrace our company culture by continuously maintaining and strengthening our relationships with our partner vendors. Open communication, training and consultations are regularly provided to our partner vendors.


Repair and Preservation Network is continually evaluating our network coverage and vendor performance. We are seeking a network of qualified industry professionals to provide skillful and timely services to our clients. Experienced and reliable contractors who meet our requirements are encouraged to apply.


To begin the process, please review requirements and complete and submit the vendor application below. Our company's Vendor Management Team will review all applications and will contact you if any further information is needed.

VENDOR REQUIREMENTS

» Insurance coverage: General Liability, Worker’s Compensation, Automobile Liability

» Successful background checks

» Computer with high speed Internet access

» Smartphone with mobile applications; digital camera
